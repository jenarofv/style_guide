Text Styles

For accesibility purposes, text sizes should always be in rem instead of pixels.

H1: Page Heading

  • text-align: center
  • font-size: 3.5rem
  • font-weight: normal
  • line-height: 46pt
  • letter-spacing: 3px
  • font-family: "Urbanist"

H2: Subheading/Section Heading

  • font-size: 2.5rem
  • font-weight: normal

Paragrahs and other text items

  • font-family: Urbanist, sans-serif OR Bitter, serif. It can be any of them, as long as I stick to it
  • font-weight: 400 (regular)
  • font-size: 1rem
